How does Intelligence work in Dota 2

Intelligence, "For the Wise, Power"

Intelligence is a primary attribute for 41 hereos within dota 2 at the moment. But, what does Intelligence do within the game?

Intelligence is used for only two things within dota 2

  • It increases your overall mana pool by 12 for each point
  • It increases your overall mana regen by 0.04 per point
  • And if your hero has the primary attribute of Intelligence it increases their physical damage by 1 point
