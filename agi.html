How does agility work in Dota 2

Agility, "You gotta go fast"

Agility is a primary attribute for 35 hereos within dota 2 at the moment. But, what does agility do within the game?

Agility is used for only two things within dota 2

  • It increases your attack speed by 1 for each point
  • It increases your overall armor by 0.14 per point
  • And if your hero has the primary attribute of agility it increases their physical damage by 1 point
