How does Strength work in Dota 2

Strength, "For the Wise, Power"

Strength is a primary attribute for 37 hereos within dota 2 at the moment. But, what does Strength do within the game?

Strength is used for only two things within dota 2

It increases your overall health by 20 for each point

It increases your overall health regen by 0.03 per point

And if your hero has the primary attribute of Strength it increases their physical damage by 1 point
